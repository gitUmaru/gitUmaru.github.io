Chill Calm

So this project was a submission for nwHacks 2020, which is one of the most popular and largest hackathon on the west coast. After find a team through the nwHacks 2020 slack channels we had a very brief brain storming session. The initial idea was to take facial input and generate computational artwork based on that. However, this quickly evolved into a very soft means of anxiety therapy.

Figure 1. Dashboard page of the web app (front end dev did really good job)

Basically, we used google cloud as our web app deploying service, and we also used their google vision API, in order to recognize emotions in a users face. The user would take a photo of their face in the brower and it automatically submits it to our API. Which we then process and generate a specific js animation based off of that. The entire thing was built in Django which was pretty hard to learn as we went along, however the challenge was appreciated.

Figure 2. Just fixed API integration issue, showing proof of concept

Some other feaures that we added were: A calming music playlist, a chat bot (so you have someone to talk to), and a button that locates mental health clinics as well as other imporant resources that are close to you

Figure 3. Example of the music page

Solid project all around, I'm really glad I did it. I enjoyed the project, especially considering that this was my first hackathon. I'm looking foreword to the next hackathon that I get to compete in.
